About Us

The Exilitay Story

Learn more about our graphics enhancement project and the team behind it.

Exilitay Graphics is a passion project created by a team of dedicated sim racing enthusiasts who wanted to push the visual boundaries of Assetto Corsa beyond what was previously possible.

Our commitment is to provide high-quality, regularly updated visual mods that work flawlessly with all Assetto Corsa content. With advanced lighting techniques and custom post-processing, Exilitay Graphics transforms the game into a photorealistic racing experience.

"I created Exilitay Graphics to combine my passion for photography, cinematography, and racing simulation. Our goal is to make every Assetto Corsa session feel like a movie scene while maintaining the integrity of the racing experience."
