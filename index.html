Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Exilitay Graphics.

How do I install Exilitay Graphics?

Installation is quick and easy! Simply drag the 'EXTENSION' and 'SYSTEM' folders into your Assetto Corsa root directory: C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\assettocorsa Important: Do not install this through Content Manager it will not work! Note: Requires at least Pure version 2.38.

Will this work with my VR Headset?

Yes, Exilitay Graphics is compatible with VR but may require some optimization for best performance. We recommend starting with the "Medium" preset and gradually increasing settings as your hardware allows. Some effects like advanced DOF may need to be disabled for optimal VR performance.

How much impact will this have on performance?

PPfilters dont impact performance but Exilitay has 3 different CSP (Custom Shaders Patch) setting presets. Medium, High, And for those who dare: Ultra.

What's the difference between the free and premium versions?

The free version includes basic visual enhancements with our standard PPFilter. Premium subscribers get access to advanced features like the custom fog system, seasonal variations, enhanced weather effects, cinematic depth of field, and regular updates. Ultimate subscribers also receive early access to development builds and custom configuration support.

Is this compatible with other mods?

Exilitay Graphics is designed to work alongside most Assetto Corsa mods, including car mods, track mods, and sound mods. However, it may conflict with other graphics or post-processing mods. We recommend not running multiple PP (Post Processing) Filters simultaneously. The mod has been specifically tested and optimized to work with Custom Shaders Patch and Pure Weather.
