Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Exilitay Graphics.

How do I install Exilitay Graphics?

Installation is straightforward: First, ensure you have Custom Shaders Patch (CSP) v0.1.79 or newer installed. Then, install Pure Weather mod version 0.075+. Finally, copy the Exilitay Realism PP filter files to your AC/system/cfg/ppfilters folder. Select the filter from the in-game PP Filter dropdown menu and enjoy!

Will this work with VR?

Yes, Exilitay Graphics is compatible with VR but may require some optimization for best performance. We recommend starting with the "Performance" preset and gradually increasing settings as your hardware allows. Some effects like advanced DOF may need to be disabled for optimal VR performance.

How much impact will this have on performance?

The performance impact varies depending on your hardware and selected settings. On recommended hardware, you can expect a 10-20% reduction in frame rate compared to vanilla settings. The mod includes multiple presets ranging from "Performance" to "Ultimate Quality" so you can find the right balance for your system.

What's the difference between the free and premium versions?

The free version includes basic visual enhancements with our standard PPFilter. Premium subscribers get access to advanced features like the custom fog system, seasonal variations, enhanced weather effects, cinematic depth of field, and regular updates. Ultimate subscribers also receive early access to development builds and custom configuration support.

Is this compatible with other mods?

Exilitay Graphics is designed to work alongside most Assetto Corsa mods, including car mods, track mods, and sound mods. However, it may conflict with other graphics or post-processing mods. We recommend not running multiple PPFilters simultaneously. The mod has been specifically tested and optimized to work with Custom Shaders Patch and Pure Weather.
